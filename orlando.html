Welcome to Orlando

Orlando is famous for its world-renowned theme parks, including Walt Disney World and Universal Studios. It attracts millions of tourists every year and offers a vibrant mix of entertainment and culture (Visit Florida, 2024).

Key Details About Orlando

  1. Population: ~310,000 (Wikipedia, 2024)
  2. Incorporated: 1875 (Wikipedia, 2024)
  3. Region: Central Florida (Visit Florida, 2024)
  4. Classification: Urban (Visit Florida, 2024)
  5. Income: Higher compared to Florida's average due to tourism and entertainment industries (Visit Florida, 2024)

Interesting Facts About Orlando

Fact Description
Theme Parks Orlando is home to Walt Disney World, Universal Studios, SeaWorld, and many more theme parks (Visit Florida, 2024).
Tourism Over 75 million visitors travel to Orlando annually, making it one of the most visited cities in the world (Wikipedia, 2024).
Convention Center Orlando has one of the largest convention centers in the United States, the Orange County Convention Center (Visit Florida, 2024).
