Welcome to Miami

Known for its stunning beaches and vibrant nightlife, Miami is a cultural hub blending Latin American influences with American traditions. It is also a gateway to the Florida Keys and the Caribbean (Visit Florida, 2024).

Key Details About Miami

  1. Population: ~470,000 (Wikipedia, 2024).
  2. Incorporated: 1896 (Wikipedia, 2024).
  3. Region: South Florida (Visit Florida, 2024).
  4. Classification: Urban (Visit Florida, 2024).
  5. Income: Higher compared to Florida's average due to real estate, tourism, and international trade (Visit Florida, 2024).

Interesting Facts About Miami

Fact Description
Beaches Miami is home to some of the most famous beaches in the world, including South Beach and Miami Beach (Visit Florida, 2024).
Nightlife Miami has a vibrant nightlife scene with numerous clubs, bars, and restaurants, making it a popular destination for party-goers (Wikipedia, 2024).
Art Deco The Art Deco district in Miami Beach is famous for its historical buildings and architecture from the 1920s-30s (Wikipedia, 2024).
