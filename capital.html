Welcome to Tallahassee

Key Details About Tallahassee

  1. Population: ~200,000 (Visit Florida, 2024).
  2. Incorporated: 1825 (Wikipedia contributors, 2024).
  3. Region: Northwest Florida (Florida State Parks, 2024).
  4. Classification: Urban (Visit Florida, 2024).
  5. Income: Moderate compared to Florida's average (Wikipedia contributors, 2024).

Interesting Facts About Tallahassee

Fact Description
State Capital Tallahassee became the capital of Florida in 1824 ( Wikipedia contributors , 2024).
University The city is home to Florida State University (FSU), a major public research university ( Florida State Parks , 2024).
Political Center As the state capital, Tallahassee hosts the government buildings and legislative sessions of Florida ( Visit Florida , 2024).
